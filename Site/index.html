Welcome

Here are Alafaya Rescue we aim to support the many animals around Alafaya that have been left out in the open. However we can suport everyone which is where you the comunity can help. Donations, volinteer work, adoptions, and fostering are a great help to both the animals in this shelter and our greater community. Why Donate
We seek to help all the animals that come our way, but we can't always support the hefty weight these animals come with. So if you have any spare change, even a penny, sending it our way can help change these poor animals' lives. Find more about donating here.
Volunteer
Here at Alafaya Rescue, we are always in need of a helping hand. Whether it is just out of the goodness of your heart or for required volunteer hours, we are glad to take all that aim to help.

Fostering

About

We are a dedicated bunch aiming to both clean up the community and care for its many inhabitants. We have been around since 2020 and have fallen in love with the area. Our goal is plain and simple: rescue as many animals as we can and find them a long-term home where they will be loved. We rescue dogs, cats, birds, and tortoises. Although we more commonly rescue dogs and cats than anything else. Going beyond just rescuing, we aim to serve our community through the mutual love of animals. We hold and join events that aim to heal hearts and bring joy. Please explore our many pages to learn more about us.
