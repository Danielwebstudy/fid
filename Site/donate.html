Thanks For your Kindness!

Our donation options include the following choices:

Penny Donation
Every donation matters, no matter how small. Unfortunately, this donation doesn't come with any special benefits, but please come on down to see how your donation has impacted our rescue.
High Value Donation
Big donations truly reach our rescue the most. They fund rent, expansion, and A/C. Additionally, donations over $30 come with a shirt and gift bag. We appreciate your generosity, and so do our many residents.
Item Donation
Have a more physical donation. We accept: toys, closed bags of food, and clean animal beds/cages.

To Make Donations

Please see our contacts page for mailing/address information. We accept cash, checks, and cards. If you still have any questions or concerns, please contact us.
